Class-Swap (temporary name)

Class-Swap is a web-app designed to streamline time/section switching classes for Columbia students. Users create accounts and list their current class(es) time/section. Then, users can search for the time/section they desire. If a match is found and confirmed by both parties, the pair are connected and are free to switch easily and painlessly.
